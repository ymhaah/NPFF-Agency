Rachel is a serif typeface designer by Danilo De Marco in 2015. The design is inspired by Bodoni typeface designed by Giambattista Bodoni and Lapiday Roman characters.

As id Bodoni font, Rachel has a strong contrast between thin and thick rods. Some letter such as “Q” and “g” are characterized by strong drawing and soft curves.

This font is free for personal and commercial use.
